
# Chart Selection Justification

## Sales Trend View

**Business Question:** How are sales changing over time?

**Chart Type:** Line Chart

**Reason:** Line charts are the most effective way to display trends across time periods.

**Design Principle Applied:** Clear trend visibility with minimal clutter.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided pie charts which cannot represent time-series information.

---

## Regional Performance View

**Business Question:** Which regions generate the highest sales and profits?

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart or Map

**Reason:** Enables direct comparison between geographic areas.

**Design Principle Applied:** Sorting regions by performance improves readability.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided 3D charts that distort comparisons.

---

## Category Profitability View

**Business Question:** Which categories contribute the most profit?

**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart or Treemap

**Reason:** Supports comparison of multiple categories and sub-categories.

**Design Principle Applied:** Profit-based color encoding improves interpretation.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided stacked charts that hide individual category performance.

---

## Customer Segment View

**Business Question:** Which customer segments drive business performance?

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Reason:** Enables simple comparison across customer groups.

**Design Principle Applied:** Consistent scaling and sorting improve interpretation.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided excessive color usage.

---

## Shipping Performance View

**Business Question:** Which shipping methods experience delays?

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Reason:** Delivery times are easily compared across shipping modes.

**Design Principle Applied:** Delay categories improve business interpretation.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided using pie charts for operational metrics.

---

## Discount vs Profit View

**Business Question:** How do discounts affect profitability?

**Chart Type:** Scatter Plot

**Reason:** Scatter plots reveal relationships and correlations between variables.

**Design Principle Applied:** Color coding by category improves insight discovery.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided aggregated charts that hide individual transaction behavior.

---

## Return Analysis View

**Business Question:** Which products or customer groups have the highest return risk?

**Chart Type:** Staked Bar Chart

**Reason:** Enables comparison of return rates across multiple dimensions.

**Design Principle Applied:** Consistent color mapping improves readability.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided tables that require excessive effort to interpret.
